About the game

Picture this: you are Moomin, and you are in Helsinki. Your girlfriend Snorkmaiden is in Ivalo, so obviously you must get there ASAP!! But how will you do that?

Today is not your lucky day: trains, buses, and direct flight from HEL to IVL are all canceled, so you must take 3 flights to get to Snorkmaiden. On top of that, you also need to take CO^2 into account, so you must choose the shortest route. You might also face some challenges along the way, but don’t be discouraged: these challenges might benefit you greatly!

Rules of the Moomin’s Traveling game are as follows:

  • Choose 3 airports between Finland and Ivalo one by one
  • Use CO^2 consciously, and try to choose the shortest route
  • Play the minigame when the chance occurs
  • If you succeed in the minigame, your CO^2 consumption reduces
  • If you choose the shortest route: yay good for you!

Rules for the minigame:

You’ll see cards that are open: the ones that have a tree on them. However, soon some of them will close. You have to remember them and choose all closed cards. If you succeed, your CO2 consumption will decrease. Hope your memory is intact!
